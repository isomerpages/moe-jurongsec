<h2>Our Learning Approach</h2>
<p>The curricula are designed to nurture our students with 21st century competencies.
All the subjects in the department are coursework-based and students are
required to be critical and inventive in their thinking to produce innovative
and realistic solutions for their coursework tasks. The lessons are designed
to engage our students through experiential learning to construct their
knowledge.</p>
<p>There are 6 subjects in Applied Studies Department, namely:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Art</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Computer Applications</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Design and Technology</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Element of Business Skills</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Food and Consumer Education / Food and Nutrition / Food Studies</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Music</p>
</li>
</ul>
