<h2>Our Learning Approach</h2>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To develop knowledge and values required of an athlete that can be applied
beyond PE.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To nurture in every JSS student a sense of physical wellbeing through
education in principles of health and fitness, thus encouraging the choice
of a healthy lifestyle through their adult life.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To create the opportunities for students to engage in competitive games
and thereby inculcating values of fair play and pursuit of excellence.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To develop an appreciation as consumers of the sports industry and nurture
an interest in furthering knowledge in the various areas of sports.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To develop an awareness of the outdoor in safety and basic injury management
to take care of oneself and others.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To develop physical competencies and beneficial lifeskills.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To provide the opportunity to stretch high-ability students to further
their development and talent in sports through the Sports Award.</p>
</li>
</ul>
